Welcome to the home of the SBCT Web Programs publishing project. Here you will find a web-friendly presentation of a range of theatrical programs from South Bend Civic Theatre productions between 2003 and 2012. Each program page features a link to the corresponding item in the SBCT Digital Program Library, an Omeka collection of scanned paper programs and accompanying metadata.
